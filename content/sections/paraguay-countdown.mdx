In 2023, Paraguay reaffirmed diplomatic ties with Taiwan, standing alone in South America against the People’s Republic of China’s campaign of economic coercion. Every day that passes without Congress enacting TPRI is another day Beijing expands port control, telecom infrastructure, and rare earth dominance across the hemisphere.
